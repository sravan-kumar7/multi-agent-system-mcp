- Evening: Enjoy a Seine River cruise.
- Approximate local transport guidance: Walk to the Louvre from the hotel or take the Metro (Line 1).
- Practical tip: Book museum tickets in advance to avoid long queues.

- Afternoon: Explore the palace and its stunning gardens.

- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the RER C train from Paris to Versailles-Château.
- Practical tip: Wear comfortable shoes for the extensive walking at Versailles.

- Morning: Visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy the panoramic views.
- Afternoon: Relax in the Champ de Mars park or visit the nearby Musée d'Orsay.
- Evening: Enjoy a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge.
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the Metro (Line 6) to Bir-Hakeim or RER C to Champ de Mars.
- Practical tip: Book Eiffel Tower tickets in advance for priority access.

### Day 5: Shopping and Departure

- Afternoon: Return to the hotel, collect luggage, and depart for the airport.
- Evening: Depart from Charles de Gaulle Airport.
- Approximate local transport guidance: Use a taxi or train (RER B) to reach the airport.
- Practical tip: Check the airport terminal and gate in advance to ensure smooth departure.
