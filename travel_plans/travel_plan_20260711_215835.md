### Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo
- Morning: Arrive at Narita or Haneda airport, take a train or bus to your hotel
- Afternoon: Check-in to your hotel, explore the nearby area (e.g., Shinjuku, Roppongi, or Ginza)
- Evening: Visit the Tokyo Tower for panoramic city views
- Approximate local transport guidance: Use the JR East train line from the airport to Shinjuku Station (approximately ¥2,000)
- Practical tip: Purchase a Suica or Pasmo card for convenient travel on public transportation

### Day 2: Tokyo Landmarks
- Morning: Visit the Meiji Shrine, a serene Shinto shrine
- Afternoon: Explore the Asakusa district, including Senso-ji Temple
- Evening: Enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner in the Ginza district
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the Tokyo Metro Ginza Line from Shinjuku to Asakusa (approximately ¥200)
- Practical tip: Learn basic Japanese phrases, such as "konnichiwa" (hello) and "arigatou" (thank you)

### Day 3: Pop Culture
- Morning: Visit the Ghibli Museum, dedicated to Studio Ghibli's anime films
- Afternoon: Explore the trendy Harajuku district, known for its fashion and snacks
- Evening: Experience the vibrant nightlife in Shibuya
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the JR Yamanote Line from Shinjuku to Harajuku (approximately ¥200)
- Practical tip: Try some local street food, such as crepes or cotton candy

### Day 4: Relaxation
- Morning: Visit the Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden for a peaceful stroll
- Afternoon: Relax at an onsen (hot spring) or a traditional Japanese bath
- Evening: Enjoy a traditional Japanese tea ceremony
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line from Shinjuku to Shinjuku Gyoen (approximately ¥200)
- Practical tip: Respect onsen etiquette, such as washing before entering the hot spring

### Day 5: Day Trip
- Morning: Take a day trip to Nikko, a UNESCO World Heritage site (approximately 2 hours away by train)
- Afternoon: Visit the Toshogu Shrine and other historical landmarks
- Evening: Return to Tokyo and enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the JR Revaty train from Shinjuku to Nikko (approximately ¥6,000 round-trip)
- Practical tip: Consider purchasing a Nikko All Area Pass for convenient travel and entry to attractions

### Day 6: Modern Tokyo
- Morning: Visit the teamLab Borderless digital art museum in Odaiba
- Afternoon: Explore the Palette Town complex, including the Venus Fort shopping mall
- Evening: Enjoy the nighttime views of Tokyo Bay
- Approximate local transport guidance: Take the Yurikamome Line from Shinjuku to Odaiba (approximately ¥400)
- Practical tip: Consider purchasing a ticket to the teamLab Borderless museum in advance to avoid long wait times

### Day 7: Departure
- Morning: Spend some last-minute time shopping for souvenirs or exploring a local market
- Afternoon: Depart for the airport and your return flight
- Approximate local transport guidance: Use the JR East train line from Shinjuku to Narita or Haneda airport (approximately ¥2,000)
- Practical tip: Check the airport's baggage restrictions and requirements before your departure
