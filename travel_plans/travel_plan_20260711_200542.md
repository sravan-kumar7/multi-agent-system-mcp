## 🗓️ Day-wise Itinerary

Here's a detailed and practical 7-day Japan trip plan from Hyderabad under ₹2 lakhs:

### 1. Travel Summary
This 7-day Japan trip plan is designed for travelers from Hyderabad, India, aiming to explore the best of Japan within a budget of ₹2 lakhs. The trip will cover major cities like Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka, offering a blend of traditional and modern Japanese experiences.

### 2. Recommended Flight Guidance
- **Departure Airport**: Hyderabad's Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD)
- **Arrival Airport**: Tokyo's Haneda Airport (HND) for easier access to the city
- **Airlines**: Consider Air India, Japan Airlines, ANA, Cathay Pacific, or Singapore Airlines
- **Booking Advice**: Book flights at least 2-3 months in advance to secure better rates. Prices can surge during cherry blossom season (March-April) and summer holidays (June-August).

### 3. Recommended Hotels
- **Budget Option**: APA Hotel or similar, with prices starting from ₹2,000-₹5,000 per night
- **Mid-range Option**: 3-star hotels like those suggested by Dimaak Tours, with prices around ₹8,000-₹9,000 per night
- **Booking Advice**: Booking in advance can help secure better rates. Consider staying in capsule hotels or budget stays for savings.

### 4. Day-wise Itinerary
**Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo**
- Arrive at Haneda Airport
- Check into your hotel
- Explore Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden
- Visit the Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building for a free view of the city

**Day 2: Tokyo**
- Visit the Meiji Shrine
- Explore Harajuku and Shibuya
- Experience the neon lights of Shinjuku

**Day 3: Tokyo to Kyoto**
- Travel to Kyoto by bullet train (Shinkansen)
- Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine
- Explore Gion, the geisha district

**Day 4: Kyoto**
- Visit the Kinkaku-ji Temple (Golden Pavilion)
- Explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
- Enjoy a traditional Kyoto dinner

**Day 5: Kyoto to Osaka**
- Travel to Osaka
- Visit the Osaka Castle
- Explore Dotonbori for nightlife and food

**Day 6: Osaka**
- Day trip to Universal Studios Japan (optional)
- Visit the Umeda Sky Building for city views
- Enjoy Osaka's food scene

**Day 7: Departure from Osaka or Return to Tokyo**
- Depending on your flight, either depart from Osaka's Kansai International Airport or return to Tokyo for your departure

### 5. Weather Advice
- Japan's weather can be humid and hot during the summer.
- Carry light, breathable clothing.
- Stay hydrated and plan for short breaks.
- Check the forecast before your trip for any rain predictions and carry an umbrella accordingly.

### 6. Estimated Budget Guidance
- **Flights**: ₹80,000 - ₹1,20,000 (round-trip)
- **Accommodation**: ₹14,000 - ₹63,000 (for 7 nights, depending on the hotel choice)
- **Food and Transportation**: ₹30,000 - ₹50,000 (depending on personal spending)
- **Activities and Entry Fees**: ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 (depending on the places visited)
- **Total**: ₹1,34,000 - ₹2,03,000

### 7. Important Travel Tips
- **Visa**: Apply for a Japanese visa well in advance. Indian citizens can use the Japan eVisa system.
- **Travel Insurance**: Consider purchasing travel insurance for unforeseen circumstances.
- **Language**: While many signs are in English, learning basic Japanese phrases can enhance your experience.
- **Respect Local Customs**: Especially when visiting temples or shrines, dress modestly and follow local etiquette.
- **Cash and Cards**: Japan is predominantly a cash society, but major cards are accepted in many places. Inform your bank of international transactions to avoid any issues.

This plan is designed to provide a balanced experience of Japan's culture, history, and entertainment, all within your budget of ₹2 lakhs. Enjoy your trip!
