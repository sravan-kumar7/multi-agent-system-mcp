## 🗓️ Day-wise Itinerary

**Travel Summary:**
A 7-day Japan trip from Hyderabad under ₹2 lakhs is a challenging but achievable goal. With careful planning, you can experience the best of Japan's culture, food, and scenery while staying within your budget.

**Recommended Flight Guidance:**
To get the best deals on flights, consider flying into Osaka (KIX) instead of Tokyo. Book your flights at least 2-3 months in advance to secure the best rates. You can use flight comparison websites like Skyscanner, Kayak, or Google Flights to find the cheapest options. Some airlines that operate from Hyderabad to Japan include Air India, Japan Airlines, ANA, Cathay Pacific, and Singapore Airlines.

**Recommended Hotels:**
For budget-friendly options, consider staying in capsule hotels or guesthouses, which can cost between ₹2,000-5,000 per night. Some popular options include:

* Capsule hotels like Nine Hours Shinjuku or Capsule Hotel Anshin Oyado
* Guesthouses like Sakura Hotel Ikebukuro or Nui.Tokyo Central Hostel
* Budget hotels like Remm Akihabara Hotel or Hotel Gajoen Tokyo

You can book your hotels through websites like Booking.com, Agoda, or Hotels.com.

**Day-wise Itinerary:**

Day 1: Arrival in Osaka

* Arrive at KIX airport and take a train to your hotel
* Visit the Dotonbori area and try some local street food
* Explore the Shinsekai district and its retro atmosphere

Day 2: Osaka

* Visit the Osaka Castle and its surrounding park
* Explore the Umeda Sky Building and its observatory deck
* Try some local cuisine like takoyaki or okonomiyaki

Day 3: Kyoto

* Take a train to Kyoto and visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine
* Explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and its scenic views
* Visit the Kinkaku-ji Temple and its beautiful gardens

Day 4: Kyoto

* Visit the Nijo Castle and its "nightingale floors"
* Explore the Gion district and its traditional Japanese architecture
* Attend a traditional tea ceremony or try some local sweets

Day 5: Tokyo

* Take a train to Tokyo and visit the Shibuya Crossing and its surrounding area
* Explore the Meiji Shrine and its peaceful atmosphere
* Visit the Tokyo Tower and its observatory deck

Day 6: Tokyo

* Visit the Asakusa district and its Senso-ji Temple
* Explore the Akihabara district and its electronic shops
* Try some local cuisine like sushi or ramen

Day 7: Departure from Tokyo

* Spend the morning shopping for last-minute souvenirs or exploring a local market
* Take a train to the airport and depart from Tokyo

**Weather Advice:**
Japan's weather can be quite unpredictable, so it's essential to pack layers and be prepared for rain or shine. The current weather in Japan is overcast clouds with a temperature of 26.95°C, and the forecast predicts light rain and overcast clouds for the next few days.

* Accommodation: ₹20,000 - ₹40,000

**Important Travel Tips:**

* Learn some basic Japanese phrases like "konnichiwa" (hello), "arigatou" (thank you), and "sumimasen" (excuse me)
* Carry a portable Wi-Fi hotspot or purchase a prepaid SIM card for data and navigation
* Try to avoid traveling during peak hours (7-9 am and 5-7 pm) to avoid crowds and long lines
* Be respectful of Japanese culture and customs, especially when visiting temples or shrines
* Don't tip at restaurants or bars, as it's not customary in Japan

By following these guidelines and being mindful of your budget, you can have a fantastic 7-day trip to Japan from Hyderabad under ₹2 lakhs. Happy travels!
