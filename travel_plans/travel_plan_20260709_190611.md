## 🗓️ Itinerary
**7-Day Trip to Vietnam Itinerary**

### Day 1: Arrival in Hanoi

* Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport (HAN) in Hanoi, Vietnam
* Transfer to your hotel in the city center (Hai Ba Trung, Ba Đinh, Dong Da, or Hoan Kiem districts)
* Spend the afternoon exploring Hanoi's Old Quarter, trying local street food, and visiting the Dong Xuan Market
* Enjoy a traditional Vietnamese dinner and watch a water puppet show at the Thang Long Water Puppet Theatre

### Day 2: Hanoi City Tour

* Visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and the Presidential Palace
* Explore the Temple of Literature, a historic university and temple complex
* Wander through the beautiful Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple
* Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and try some of Hanoi's famous dishes, such as bun cha or pho
* Visit the Museum of Ethnology to learn about Vietnam's diverse cultures

### Day 3: Ha Long Bay

* Take a day trip to Ha Long Bay, a stunning natural wonder with over 1,600 limestone islands and islets
* Cruise through the emerald waters and enjoy the breathtaking scenery
* Visit caves, grottos, and hidden lagoons, and go kayaking or swimming in the bay
* Enjoy a seafood lunch on the boat and watch the sunset over the bay

### Day 4: Ninh Binh

* Take a day trip to Ninh Binh, a province known for its natural beauty and historic sites
* Visit the Trang An Grottoes, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and take a boat ride through the caves and tunnels
* Explore the ancient capital of Hoa Lu, with its temples and ruins
* Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and try some of Ninh Binh's specialties, such as goat meat or fish sauce

### Day 5: Sapa

* Take an overnight train to Sapa, a mountain town in northern Vietnam
* Trek through the beautiful landscapes and villages of the Sapa Valley
* Visit the Sapa Market and try some local handicrafts and souvenirs
* Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant and try some of Sapa's specialties, such as grilled pork or fish

### Day 6: Sapa Trekking

* Spend the day trekking through the Sapa Valley, visiting villages and meeting local hill tribes
* Enjoy the stunning scenery and take in the fresh mountain air
* Visit the Silver Waterfall and enjoy a picnic lunch in the countryside
* Return to Sapa in the evening and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant

### Day 7: Return to Hanoi

* Take the overnight train back to Hanoi
* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or visiting any last-minute sights
* Depart for the airport and fly back home
